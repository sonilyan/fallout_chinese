1:After the fall of the Jackals, the gangsters of Rainbow made an attempt to take over the military bases in Radius. But without heavy weaponry they were unable to succeed.
2:Due to the general lack of firearms in Radius, violent methods of resolving conflicts went out of fashion. More peaceful solutions became prevalent.
3:Trade and mutual cooperation flourished, leading to impressive economical development. Rainbow soon became famous for its casinos and brothels.
